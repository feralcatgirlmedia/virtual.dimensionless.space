CYBERIA is an experience that challenges the conventions of what a rave can be. We stray away from the hyper-eurocentric, closed-off norms of modern DJ scenes and focus on the up-and-coming, what's hot right now, by giving marginalized artists a platform to show off their skills on the decks and a place for people to build true, lasting community. With no genre or setting limitations, CYBERIA is the true sound of the Tkaronto underground.

CYBERIA is as ever-evolving as the people who play and the people who attend, and takes many forms, with most events focusing on outdoor, low-cost events, but plenty more is in the works behind the scenes.

CYBERIA is by outcasts, for outcasts. Harassment will not be tolerated at these events, and targeting someone based on race, religion, gender, sexuality is grounds for an immediate lifetime ban from all CYBERIA events.
